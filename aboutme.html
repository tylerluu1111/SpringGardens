About Us

Spring Garden

Our Story

Spring Gardens Nursery was started in 1993 by owner Richard Gieseke, who has been in the landscape and nursery business for over 30 years. We've been in business selling wholesale plants and trees to professional landscapers; we are now open to the general public as well. Whether you're a part of a landscape company that needs supplies for a job, or a homeowner who wants a fresh look for your yard, we at Spring Gardens are ready and equipped to meet your needs! We are able to deliver plants directly to your home or business.

owner

Daniel Gomez-Bilbao, owner of Spring Gardens, combines his love for plants with a commitment to excellence. With a keen eye for quality and a passion for horticulture, he carefully nurtures each plant to the highest standards of care.

Manager

Our manager, with extensive plant care expertise, actively supports the team and customers at the garden center, offering valuable guidance to maintain the highest standards of service, knowledge, and customer experience.

Team

Our members are the backbone of the garden, dedicated to creating and maintaining vibrant spaces. Each brings unique skills and a passion for horticulture, ensuring every plant is carefully nurtured with precision, expertise, and attention to detail.
